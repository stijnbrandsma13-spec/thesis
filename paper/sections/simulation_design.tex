\subsection{Data Generating Processes}
\label{sec:dgp}
For the DGP I will probably implement a DGP that follows \eqref{eq:rcmlinear}, \eqref{eq:swamyrep}.
Maybe I find another type of RCM that either suits the problem better, or is just interesting to study.
In that case I might simulate such a DGP as well. An example could be a binary choice model.

Most interest lies in the distributions for $\bm{\beta}_{it}$.
I will let $\bm{\beta}_{it}$ have the same distribution as in \citet{FoxKimRyanBajari2011} for nice comparability.
This is a mixture distribution of two normals. 
I might also try to estimate a couple more different distributions, but I have not yet thought about what distributions might be interesting.
